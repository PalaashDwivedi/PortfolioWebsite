About Me

I'm a passionate game developer currently in my third year of college, with a strong focus on creating original and mechanically rich gaming experiences. My journey in game development has been driven by a fascination with gameplay systems and the challenge of building mechanics that feel both engaging and unique.

My core strength lies in building gameplay systems that emphasise tight controls, innovative mechanics, and player engagement. I work extensively with Unreal Engine, handling everything from scripting and level design to creating custom animations that enhance gameplay feedback and visual style.

What sets me apart is my hands-on approach to learning and my ability to quickly adapt to new tools and programming languages. I believe in learning through practice, which is why I've built several game prototypes that showcase different aspects of game development.

When I'm not coding or designing levels, I'm exploring new game mechanics, studying what makes games feel great to play, and always looking for that next creative challenge.
